Features

Everything you need to plan, climb, and improve—without the clutter.

AR Route Tracing

Use your camera to visualize and trace beta on the wall. Capture sequences, holds, and moves to revisit later.

  • Overlay moves on real routes
  • Record attempts with timestamps
  • Share beta with friends

Route Finder

Discover problems and routes by grade, style, or gym. Build your ticklist before you arrive.

  • Filter by grade, style, and setter
  • Maps for participating gyms
  • Save to wishlist

Log Your Climbs

Fast, structured logging for boulders and sport. Track volume, grade, and time‑on‑wall to see trends.

  • One‑tap send/attempts
  • Session summaries & charts
  • Offline first—sync later

Community Forum

Ask for beta, share progress, and connect with climbers near you.

  • Topic channels by gym and grade
  • Media posts & reactions
  • Report & moderation tools

Leaderboards & Rankings

Friendly competition that motivates. Compare with friends or your gym community.

  • Weekly and all‑time boards
  • Custom clubs and groups
  • Anti‑spam & fair‑play checks

About GoClimb

GoClimb is a student‑built project focused on clean, fast tools that help climbers plan better sessions and improve steadily. We're climbers first, so features are designed from the gym floor up.
