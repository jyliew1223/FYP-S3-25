Features

Everything you need to plan, climb, and improve—built for the climbing community.

AR Experience

Bring your climbing sessions to life with AR. Use your camera to view routes, study the flow of moves, and understand how each hold connects. You can place the model in your space, look at different angles, and review the recommended beta before trying the climb.

What you can do:

  • View route lines and hold placements through AR
  • Place 3D crags in your real environment
  • Switch between available routes and compare sequences
  • Explore beta visually before your climb
  • Save your routes for offline use

Route Finder

Browse crags and routes with ease. Whether you're planning a session or just exploring new places to climb, the Route Finder helps you see what's available at each crag and what grade range you're looking at.

What you can do:

  • Browse routes by crag
  • View grades, details, and difficulty
  • Check locations through the map
  • Save routes for offline access

Log Your Climbs

Track your climbs in a simple, structured way. Record your attempts, note how each climb felt, and keep a history of what you've sent. Your logbook grows with you, making it easy to look back on improvement over time.

What you can do:

  • Log climbs with attempts, date, and completion status
  • Add notes and title each session
  • Browse your past logs anytime
  • Works offline and syncs when connected

Community Forum

Connect with climbers, share your sessions, and ask for beta from others. The forum is your space to post photos, talk about climbs, and join conversations tagged by crag or topic.

What you can do:

  • View and create posts
  • Add images to share your climbs
  • Like and comment as a member
  • Explore posts through tags & categories
  • Guests can browse the feed before signing up

Leaderboards & Rankings

See how you stack up in the community. Rankings help keep your sessions fun and rewarding — track your points from logged climbs and follow weekly, monthly, and all-time leaderboards.

What you can do:

  • View leaderboards by time period
  • Track your total points from logged climbs
  • Compare progress with other climbers
  • Personal profile shows your stats and activity

About GoClimb

GoClimb is a student‑built project focused on clean, fast tools that help climbers plan better sessions and improve steadily. We're climbers first, so features are designed from the gym floor up.
